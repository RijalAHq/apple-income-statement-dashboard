# Apple Income Statement Dashboard (2021–2025)

### Power BI Financial Analysis Project

This project presents an interactive financial analysis dashboard of Apple Inc.'s Income Statement from fiscal year 2021 to 2025 using Power BI. The dashboard focuses on revenue growth, profitability trends, margin performance, and cost structure analysis to provide meaningful business insights into Apple's financial performance over a five-year period.

---

## Project Overview

The project was developed using Power BI to transform financial statement data into an interactive dashboard that supports financial analysis and decision-making.

The analysis focuses on:

* Revenue Performance
* Profitability Analysis
* Margin Analysis
* Cost Structure Analysis
* Income Statement Reporting

The objective of this project is to evaluate Apple's financial performance and identify trends in revenue generation, operational efficiency, and profitability between 2021 and 2025.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Growth

Apple's revenue increased from **$365.8 billion in 2021** to **$416.2 billion in 2025**, demonstrating the company's ability to maintain long-term growth despite temporary declines in 2023 and slower growth in 2024.

### Profitability Improvement

Gross Profit Margin improved from **41.8% in 2021** to **46.9% in 2025**, indicating stronger profitability and more efficient management of production and operating costs.

### Cost Structure Efficiency

Cost of Revenue represented a declining proportion of total sales throughout the period, contributing to improved gross profit generation and stronger overall financial performance.

### Operating Performance

Operating Income remained consistently strong and reached **$133.1 billion in 2025**, reflecting Apple's ability to manage operating expenses while continuing to invest in research and development.

### Earnings Growth

Net Income reached a record **$112.0 billion in 2025**, supported by improved cost efficiency, healthy operating margins, and sustained business growth.
